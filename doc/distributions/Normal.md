Normal distribution
===================
Normal(Double **mean**, Double **sd**)
--------------------------------------

The normal probability distribution.

### Parameters

- Double **mean** - the mean of the distribution.
- Double **sd** - the standard deviation of the distribution.

### Return type

- Double
